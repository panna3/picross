Tamaki's Picross (Recommand using firefox.)

How to play:
1. The number on the top is the number of solid in each column. Left click to make squares as solid.
 2. The number on the left is the number of solid in each row.
 3. Shift + click to make as empty so that you know where to not make squares as solid.
 4. You can select multiple squares by dragging your mouse after click.
Shift + move your mouse is for multiple selection of empty squares.
5. When you complete a picture with the solid squares, you will see a name of picture on the left side.
